Count Data: Subgroup vs Population Method Comparison

Analysis

2025-12-04

Overview

This document applies both the population method and the subgroup method to the first dataset from scenario 2 of the count endpoint simulation, to verify that the calculations are correct.

Why scenario 2? Scenario 2 has heterogeneity only in x_4 (three levels), with no effect in x_4=a and enhanced effects in x_4=b and x_4=c.This simpler structure allows better comparison between theoretical truth and observed estimates, unlike scenario 5 which has complex interactions across all 10 covariates.

Conclusion

This document demonstrates:

  1. Population method: Fits one model to all data, replicates estimate across subgroups
  2. Subgroup method: Fits separate models within each subgroup
  3. Comparison: Shows how much the subgroup estimates vary from the population estimate

The variation between subgroup and population estimates represents the heterogeneity in treatment effects across subgroups, plus sampling variability.
